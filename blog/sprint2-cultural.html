Strengths – I persevere and I'm very hard-working and reliable, which makes me a good team-player.

Limitations – perfectionism (spending too much time trying to make things right), imposter syndrome (feeling like I don't belong), not backing myself and skills enough, always feeling that I don't know enough.

To maintain my health and wellbeing in order to learn and work effectively with others, I will do my best to sleep well, exercise, and maintaining a healthy diet. I will work on my perfectionist tendencies and try to remember that it's about learning, rather than getting everything perfect and 'right'. Commitment to seeking help in a timely way

  • try to solve the problem myself, research on internet (1 hour)
